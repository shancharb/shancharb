- Healthy & Happy Lifestyle -

sweat · smile · repeat

Living a healthy lifestyle is a combination of many different elements. It includes a positive attitude, happy mindset, regular physical activity, healthy eating, proper sleep, and much more. This is something that is important, especially for University students who can very easily be overcome by a mental, physical, or emotional illness while at school. It is important to maintain awareness about the health of your body in order to reach success.

Here is a look at a sample leg workout routine that would be perfect for a young woman looking to gain muscle, burn fat, and improve strength & endurance. It is important to warm up prior to training any muscle group with light cardio and dynamic stretches. The workout should also be followed by static stretching to avoid muscle injury. Each exercise should be done with a weight that is difficult, but still allows the number of repetitions to be completed.

- Leg Day Workout Routine -

Exercise Repetition Sets
Barbell Squats 10-12 5
Deadlifts 10-12 5
Lunges 12-15 3
Leg Extension 12-15 3
Hamstring Curl 12-15 3
Calf Raises 12-15 3

It is extremely important to perform these exercises with proper form to avoid straining joints or muscles, as well as to maximize the benefits. Click here for videos to further describe each exercise with instructions.
